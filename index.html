Kazi Nazrul Islam

The national poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Poet

Kazi Nasrul Islam is the national poet of Bangladesh who is especially recognized as Bidrohi Kobi(rebel poet) around the country. Nazrul is a famous poet Nazrul's writing deal with the revolution of that period of the British government. His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangladesh. He hass raise his voice against any kind of fascism and oppression and got popularity swiftly around the Indian Subcontinent.

Awesome Writer

Nazrul's writing explored themes such as love, freedom, humanity and revolution. He opposed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism, including religious, caste-based and gender-based. Through out his career, Nazrul wrote short stories, novels and essays but is best known for his songs and poems. He pioneered new music forms such as Bengali ghazals. Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songs (many recorded on HMV and gramaphone records), collectively known as Nazrul Geeti.

"Love has no meaning or amount."-Kazi Nazrul Islam
